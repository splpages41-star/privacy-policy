Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Sea Prince Logistics (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Sea Prince Logistics – Instant Quotation mobile app and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

Information We Collect

  • Contact details (e.g., name, email address, phone number).
  • Shipment details (e.g., origin/destination, weight, value, service type).
  • Attachments you upload to support your quotation (e.g., PDFs).
  • Usage data such as device type, app interactions, and diagnostics.

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate instant quotations and share them with you and our operations team.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve app reliability, performance, and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the Service (e.g., email delivery, hosting).
  • Logistics partners when needed to prepare or fulfill your shipment request.
  • Authorities if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Update or correct your contact details by contacting us.
  • Request access or deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Control optional analytics in your device settings where available.

Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and revise the “Last updated” date above.
